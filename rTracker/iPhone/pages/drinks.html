Design and use your own drinks tracker with rTracker

The Drinks tracker is a simple log for caffeinated and alcoholic drinks consumption, and computes daily caffeine, daily alcohol and weekly alcohol intake. Just tap the checkbox and save for each drink throughout the day.

If these aren't your usual drinks, pick your own and modify the tracker to match your preferences. Choice is what rTracker is all about.
